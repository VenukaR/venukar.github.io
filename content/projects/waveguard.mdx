## WaveGuard

A Flutter-based mobile application designed to empower communities to protect and preserve marine ecosystems through awareness and reporting features.

### Key Features

- **Marine Ecosystem Monitoring**: Track and report marine environmental issues
- **Community Engagement**: Connect users passionate about ocean conservation
- **Reporting System**: Submit observations and concerns about marine habitats
- **Awareness Campaigns**: Educational content about marine preservation
- **Location-Based Features**: Report issues specific to geographic areas

### Technology Stack

- Flutter for cross-platform mobile development
- Dart programming language
- Real-time data synchronization
- Geolocation services
- Cloud-based backend

### Mission

Empowering coastal communities and ocean advocates to actively participate in marine conservation efforts through technology-driven awareness and collaborative reporting.

### Impact

Facilitates collective action for protecting marine biodiversity and raising awareness about threats to ocean ecosystems.
