- **Past Paper Library**: Extensive collection of previous examination papers

- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor performance and improvement over time

### Technology Stack

- Web-based platform for accessibility
- SMS integration for notifications
- Forum system for peer interaction
- Database for question banks
- Analytics for performance tracking

### Innovation

Combines traditional exam preparation with modern communication methods, ensuring all students can access resources regardless of connectivity limitations.

### Accessibility Focus

SMS-based updates ensure students in areas with limited internet access can still receive important notifications and participate in the learning ecosystem.
